Ethical Issues

Privacy and Data Security: Ethical web development ensures the protection of user data and privacy. Transparency and Honesty: Being transparent and honest in practices, avoiding deceptive actions. Intellectual Property: Respecting and not infringing upon intellectual property rights. Avoiding Harm: Ensuring content and practices do not cause harm or offend diverse cultural backgrounds.

Accessibility Issues

Inclusivity: Ensuring that websites are designed and coded to be accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. Design & Code: Incorporating features that support screen readers, alternative text for images, and keyboard navigation. Legal Compliance: Adhering to standards like the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) to meet legal requirements. User Experience: Enhancing the user experience by providing options for different accessibility needs, such as text size adjustments or color contrast options.
